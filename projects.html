V - Information Exchange Management System (V-IEMS)
Feb 2018 - May 2018

V – IEMS aims to serve as a common web based platform for student interaction and knowledge sharing for students of the institute. V-IEMS is a website which allows students of the institute to post information on a common feed, ask queries/doubts to other students on Q&A section, etc. It also allows teaching staff of the institute to conduct timed mock tests for students for their practice.
